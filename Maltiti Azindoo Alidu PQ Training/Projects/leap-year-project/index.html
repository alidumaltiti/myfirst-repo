Is it a Leap Year?

Enter a year and see whether it's a leap year — with the reasoning.

Flowchart

flowchart TD
  A[Start] --> B[Input year]
  B --> C{Is year % 4 == 0?}
  C -- No --> F[Not a leap year]
  C -- Yes --> D{Is year % 100 == 0?}
  D -- No --> E[Leap year]
  D -- Yes --> G{Is year % 400 == 0?}
  G -- Yes --> E[Leap year]
  G -- No --> F[Not a leap year]
  E --> H[Output "Leap year"]
  F --> I[Output "Not a leap year"]
  H --> J[End]
  I --> J[End]
