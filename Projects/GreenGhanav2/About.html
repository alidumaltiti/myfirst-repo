Why Green Skills Matter

Green skills are the knowledge, abilities, values, and attitudes needed to live, work, and thrive in ways that protect and restore the environment. From renewable energy and sustainable agriculture to waste management and conservation, these skills are essential for building resilient communities and a climate-smart economy.
